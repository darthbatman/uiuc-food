About

The following is a data exploration of all restaurants, cafes, etc. in the UIUC extended campus. The project was inspired by attempting to eat at all the eateries local to the Champaign-Urbana area while attending the University of Illinois. The visualization was inspired by one of Professor Wade Fagen-Ulmschneider's data discoveries: Every Gen Ed at UIUC, by GPA.

Dataset

The dataset used for this project was compiled through the use of webscraping for eatery names and addresses, geocoding to convert the addresses to coordinates, and using the Zomato API to obtain price and rating information. The dataset was filtered by removing fast food eateries by cross checking a separate dataset of American fast food chains and then further filtered by checking whether an eatery fell in the bounds defined as UIUC campus and extended campus. UIUC campus and extended campus were defined via the creation of polygons in Google My Maps, accounting for Campustown, Downtown Champaign, etc. Bounds checking was accomplished by exporting a KML file from Google My Maps and then performing point-in-polygon checks.
