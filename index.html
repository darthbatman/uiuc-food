About

The following is a data exploration of all restaurants, cafes, etc. in the UIUC extended campus. The project was inspired by attempting to eat all the eateries local to the Champaign-Urbana area while attending the University of Illinois. The visualization was inspired by one of Professor Wade Fagen-Ulmschneider's data discoveries: Every Gen Ed at UIUC, by GPA.
